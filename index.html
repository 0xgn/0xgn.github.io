Our Products

Oxgn sells organic and ethically-sourced coffee, cacao, chocolate, and herbal teas.

We travel to source Oxgn products in their natural environment from cooperatives near farms, jungles, and volcanoes with nutrient-rich soil to bring you the purest, highest quality selection available. Our products are grown and assembled at their origin and delivered to you fresh.

All the products that we sell are good for your body as they are rich in nutrients, good for the environment as they are produced organically and sustainably, and good for the community that grows them as they are ethically-sourced.

If you're interested in buying our products or discussing wholesale opportunities, please reach out to us.

Our Partners

We work with a network of farmers, cooperatives, and artisans that produce ethically and organically, and we help them with online sales and international export.

Our partners are our friends, and share our values that food and drink must be good for the people that consume them, good for the environment where they are produced, and good for the community that helps them grow.

If you're interested in becoming a partner, please get in contact with us.
